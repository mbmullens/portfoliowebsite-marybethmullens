My Story

I grew up in Chicago with an aptitude for drawing and painting. This led to my Bachelor’s Degree in Art and Graphic Design from Eastern Illinois University. After college, my husband and I moved to Indianapolis where I've worked for various small businesses on their in-house design teams, as well as freelance designed for several non-profits.

For the past decade, I brought my skills to Marigold Clothing, a boutique in Indianapolis. As their Marketing Manager, I was responsible for promotions across all channels to increase customer satisfaction and revenue. Some highlights from my time there include refreshing their branding, elevating their photography, re-designing their website in WordPress and then launching an ecommerce site on Shopify.

Recently I went back to school through Career Foundry to update my tech skills, learning how to enhance a website or mobile app with best best practices in User Experience, User Interface, and Front-End Development. The goal is to elevate my career with a new Senior Graphic Design position or junior role in UX/UI and Web Design.

I’m eager to bring my blend of creativity, empathy and strategic thinking to any company or organization. I am open to full-time, contract-to-hire or freelance jobs in Indianapolis plus remote jobs anywhere in the U.S.

Join me on an exciting journey of breathing new life into projects and setting high standards in design and user engagement!
