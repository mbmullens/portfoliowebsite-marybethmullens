About Me

My Story

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Mary Beth Mullens, and I'm a Graphic, Marketing and UX/UI Designer from Indianapolis.

With two decades in visual communication, I've had the pleasure of enhancing many different brands, ensuring products shine, and delivering genuine human-centered experiences. I bring years of trained artistic skills, a love of problem solving and a caring approach to any role!

My background includes growing up in Chicago before receiving a Bachelor’s Degree in Art and Graphic Design from Eastern Illinois University in 2004. Afterwards my husband and I moved to Indy, and I have since worked at in-house design teams and freelance design for various small businesses and non-profits around the city.

As Marketing Director for the boutique Marigold Clothing for the past ten years, I was responsible for engaging promotions across all channels that increased customer satisfaction and revenue. Some of the highlights from my time there included refreshing their brand design, elevating the look of their photography and helping launch their ecommerce website on Shopify.

Recently I updated my tech skills through award-winning online school Career Foundry, learning how to enhance a website or app with best practices in User Experience, User Interface and Basic Front-End Development. The goal is to take the next step into a new Senior Graphic Design position or entry-level role in UI/UX and Website Design. I am open to full-time, contract-to-hire jobs in Indianapolis plus remote jobs anywhere in the U.S.

I’m eager to bring my unique blend of creativity and strategic thinking to any company or organization. Join me on an exciting journey of breathing new life into projects and setting high standards in design, accessibility and user engagement!
