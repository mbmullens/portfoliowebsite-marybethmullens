About

My Story

My name is Mary Beth Mullens, and I’m a Graphic and UI/UX Designer from Indianapolis with two decades in visual communication. I transform marketing ideas into compelling creations for print and online realms.

My background includes growing up in Chicago before receiving my Bachelor’s Degree in Art in 2004 from Eastern Illinois University, specializing in Graphic Design. Since then, I moved to Indianapolis, designing for various small businesses throughout my career. My trained artistic skills, love of problem solving and caring approach has allowed me to enhance many brands, ensure products shine, and deliver human-centered experiences.

As Marketing Director for the boutique Marigold Clothing for ten years, I was responsible for engaging promotions across all channels that increased customer satisfaction and revenue. Some of the highlights from my time there included refreshing their branding, elevating the look of their photography and helping launch their ecommerce website on Shopify.

Recently I updated my tech skills through the online school CareerFoundry, learning how to enhance a website or web app through User Experience, User Interface and Basic Front-End Development. The goal is to take the next step into a new Senior Graphic Design position or entry-level role in UI/UX and Website Design. I am open to full-time, contract-to-hire jobs in Indianapolis plus remote jobs anywhere in the U.S. I’m eager to bring my unique blend of creative, strategic, empathetic thinking to any company or organization.

Join me on an exciting journey of breathing life into new projects and setting high standards in design, accessibility and user engagement.
