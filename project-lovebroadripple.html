Case Study: Love, Broad Ripple

neighborhood association branding

Brand Guideline Design for Love, Broad Ripple, a non-profit coalition founded by neighborhood merchants in Indianapolis.

Quicklinks: About | Logo | Icon | Colors | Typography | Campaigns | Social Media

about:

Love, Broad Ripple is a 501(c)(6) foundation comprised of Broad Ripple Village businesses working together to cultivate a vibrant community, promote local merchants and raise funds for neighborhood growth opportunities.

logo:

The word 'Love' is styled to look like personal handwriting since the idea behind the name is sending a love letter of support to the area from the business owners.
Multiple formats of horizontal, vertical, one-color and multicolor were developed for versatility.

icon:

A simplified icon incorporates the neighborhood initials 'BR' with a location marker and a hand-sketched heart (to tie back into the Love font).

colors:

This merchant group is an off-shoot of the primary neighborhood organization Broad Ripple Village Association. As such, the BRVA's color pallete was utilized to promote a shared neighborhood identity.

typography:

The brush font Hello August was chosen to deliver a distinct and artistic vibe which the community is known for.

campaigns: village vibes

'Village Vibes' is the first fundraising campaign launched by Love, Broad Ripple to introduce special merch, food and beer that celebrates the neighborhood with proceeds donated to the foundation.

The Village Vibes retro-inspired logo is inspired by Broad Ripple's long cultural history, and reminds everyone it's still a unique, fun, safe area welcoming to all.

social media

Love, Broad Ripple's goal is to celebrate and protect the long-term vision of Broad Ripple. Their social media messaging invites people to visit the community, support local and enjoy the good vibes.
